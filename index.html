EVA: AI-Driven Avatar for College Event Promotion

Authors: Anitta Kurian, Reuben Mathew Tharakan, Keerthana Dhanish, Sravan Chand, Priya C V

College media teams spend hours creating consistent promotional content for every event. EVA automates this: given a text prompt, it generates an event poster featuring a personalised avatar (consistent appearance across all images) along with a context-aware caption. Under the hood, we fine-tuned Stable Diffusion v1.5 with DreamBooth and LoRA on institution-specific imagery, and used a GPT-based LLM for caption generation. In real-world tests during technical fests and cultural events, the design team saw a 75% reduction in design time.

VOSK: Voice-Assisted Multilingual Smart Kiosk System

Authors: Sravan Chand, Keerthana Dhanish, Anitta Kurian, Reuben Mathew Tharakan, Lekshmi R

Railway stations and public spaces in India rely heavily on manual help desks, inaccessible to elderly users, non-English speakers, and people unfamiliar with touchscreens. VOSK replaces that with a voice-first kiosk: speak a query in English, Hindi, or Malayalam, and get a spoken answer back. The pipeline chains Whisper and AI4Bharat IndicASR for speech recognition, SentenceTransformer embeddings for intent detection, and HiFi-GAN neural TTS for the spoken response. The system achieves 95% language identification accuracy and ~90% intent classification across real railway queries.
